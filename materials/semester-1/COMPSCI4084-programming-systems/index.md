## Unix - Week 4 and Week 5

### Why Unix is Important
Unix has powerful scripting languages called shells for automating routine data analysis. Bash scripting is widely used in cybersecurity and complements C programming. Unix/Linux is preferred for ethical hackers, penetration testers, and security analysts.

## Assessment Info and Instructions

### Assessment Overview
| Assessment Type | Weight | Date |
|----------------|--------|------|
| Participation to peer review (tAPP) | 5% | Weekly |
| Assessed Exercise 1 (Python) | 10% | 03/10/2025 |
| Assessed Exercise 2 (Python) | 15% | 10/10/2025 |
| Assessed Exercise 3 (Java OR Unix) | 15% | 24/10/2025 |
| Programming Assessment (invigilated) | 15% | 31/10/2025 |
| Lab Exam | 40% | December 2025 |

### Participation to Team Application Exercises (Week 1 to Week 5) - 5%
Team problem-solving exercises each week during lecture timeslots on Fridays from 1 to 3 pm.

### Weekly Lab Assessments

#### Lab Exercise Week 2 Python (10%) - Assessment 1 - 3rd October 2025
20 questions based on week 1 and the first session on week 2
Duration: 1 hour

#### Lab Exercise Week 3 Python (15%) - 10 October 2025  
30 Questions based on Week 2 and week 3 (Python)
Duration: 1 hour

#### Lab Exercise Week 5 - Java (15%) - 24 October 2025
30 questions based on Java
Duration: 1 hour

#### Lab Exercise Week 5 - Unix (15%) - 24 October 2025
Assessment for Unix track students

#### Lab Exercise Week 6 - Programming Assessment (15%) - 31 October 2025
Duration: 1 hour
Instructions:
- Three questions: One Python, one Java, one Unix question
- Each student must select only two of them
- Cybersecurity students MUST do Unix and Java or Python
- CS and DS students can choose any two questions
